Welcome to the Bonefish and Tarpon Trust Tarpon Fish Tracker!
Here, you will find the detections of tarpon tagged with acoustic transmitters. The Tarpon Acoustic Tagging Project is a collaborative, five-year program designed to broaden our understanding of tarpon movement and habitat uses. The results will help shape future conservation measures, including the protection of critical habitats and improvements to fishing regulations.

  • Click on a transmitter ID on the left side of the screen and see where this fish was first detected
  • Each circle indicates the detection in its sequence
  • Click again on a bubble to see additional information on the fish
  • Click on the arrow to see the next detection of the fish
  • Zoom in and out to see the fishes movement pattern
