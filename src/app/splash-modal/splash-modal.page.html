Welcome to the Bonefish and Tarpon Trust Tarpon Fish Tracker!
Here, you will find the detections of Atlantic tarpon tagged with acoustic transmitters. The Tarpon Acoustic Tagging Project is being conducted by Bonefish & Tarpon Trust and University of Massachusetts Amherst, along with other partners collaborating in the sharing of telemetry data. This 5+ year project is designed to broaden our understanding of local and regional tarpon movements and related habitat uses. Results from this project will help shape future conservation measures, including the protection of critical habitats and improvements to fishing regulations.

  • Click on a transmitter ID on the left side of the screen to see the fish's detections
  • The yellow line is the connection across sequential detection and is represented "as the crow flies"
  • Click on the tarpon icon to see additional information on the particular fish
  • Control the FishTracker and its speed using the buttons in the upper right corner
  • Zoom in and out to see the fish's movement pattern
